About

When talking about “novels,” many people think of thick books. that comes with many letters that can be read until the eyes are dizzying And it takes a long time to finish, but now we are in the digital age. The style of reading novels is therefore different from before. You don't have to carry heavy books in your bag. All you need is a smartphone to read it. Whether it's from an E-Book, from an app to read various novels.And the hottest thing right now is the “BKD” app or BKD, a new type of chat novel reading app. The sister site of the Thanwalai novel reading website under the company Ookbee U, a joint venture between Ookbee and Tencent.


The interest of the Joylada app lies in the "chat novels" which will make readers feel like reading chats. Easy to read, fluent reading, and doesn't take long. And it's easy to use. Within the app, there will be categories of novels into different categories, such as Pink Road, love, romance,


Gray sea, drama, sadness, heartbreak, and fun house, comedy, humor, etc. Readers can adjust the speed of the chat or novel to 3 levels: slow, normal, and fast, where the automatic chat will flow continuously. Without the reader having to slide the screen until their fingers get tired. But if you can't read in time, you can tap the screen to stop.
